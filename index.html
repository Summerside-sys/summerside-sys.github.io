Condo FAQ

 Below are some member Frequently Asked Questions this is not a comprehensive list, more will be added as they arise...

1. Q. Does Summerside Association Inc have a facebook page?
A. Yes, please come and friend us on our Facebook page.

2. Q. How do I pay my monthly maintenance fee, with my bank's auto bill pay service?
A. You should specify to your bank SUMMERSIDE ASSOCIATION, INC as the payee. And the payment check should be mailed to: Gulf Coast Community Management, c/o CenterState Bank, P.O. BOX 21586, Tampa, Florida. 33622. You'll need to include your Account Number [i.e. SUM1234] This identifier is available on your paystub coupon book mailing that you should have recieved in the mail at the beginning of March this year. You should also include in your check memo your last name and condo address, i.e. 'For SMITH at 1234 Summerside' for reference

3. Q. When is garbage pick up?
A. Garbage pick up for our complex is currently on Mondays and Thursdays for dumpster pickup and Fridays for recycling (Can be Saturday if Friday is missed). After January 6th, 2020, all recycling will be picked up from the BLUE, multi-material containers. For more information on how to appropriately recycle visit Sarasota Country Waste Management or download the How-to Guide listed above.

4. Q. What do I do with larger oversized garbage items?
A. Oversized items can be dropped off for landfill at 4010 Knights Trail Rd. Nokomis, Florida 34275, Monday thru Friday 8am - 4:30pm For more information on disposing of larger items visit Sarasota Country Waste Management or call 941-493-4100

5. Q. What is the keycode to the pool bathroom?
A. For security reasons the keycodes change frequently. Please contact the community Management Company: Gulf Coast Community Management 941-870-5600 for door keycodes

6. Q. I would like to rent my condo out. What protocol do I need to follow?
A. An application with a criminal background check must be submitted 30 days prior to moving in along with a $50 dollar application fee. No lease can be less than 30 days. Summerside Association Board or Property Management will notify the landlord in the case of denial.

7. Q. I'm a pet owner. Am I allowed to have my pet in my condo?
A. You are allowed to have one cat and or one SMALL dog no larger than 15 inches high. County ordinance states that all dogs must be on a leash and pet owners are responsible for picking up after their pet's waste. There is a dog run on the west side of the property along the fence for walking dogs.

8. Q. I'm a member but I missed the last Summerside Association meeting? Can I receive a copy of the meeting minutes so I can see what was discussed / approved?
A. Click here to request copies of meeting minutes when they become available.
